My favourite places at IIT KGP

The Indian Institute of Technology (IIT) Kharagpur is widely regarded as one of the most beautiful and aesthetically designed college campuses in India. Spanning over 2,100 acres, the campus is designed in the shape of a horseshoe and surrounded by lush green trees and rolling hills. The campus boasts of several iconic structures including the Rajendra Mishra School of Engineering Entrepreneurship, the central library, and the Gymkhana. The institute has won several awards for its excellent infrastructure and architecture, and has been praised for its blend of traditional and modern design elements.
